LONDON FOOD SPOTS — where to go in London to eat like a local
MONOHON RAMEN /
Best ramen in London without a doubt, forget the places that make you line up like a fool, this ramen is legitimate. Also, the draught beer is that perfect temperature of just above freezing.
DAQUISE /
Old school heavy and delicious Polish comfort food for a cold London day. The place has been around since before Poland came out from the Soviet bloc, and the decor looks like it belongs there.
OKAN /
This anassuming little Japanese eatery sits right in the heart of Brixton market. This is the place to come to for okonomiyaki, which is essentially a hearty Japanese pizza. Cold beers too, and a nice little atmoshphere inside.
CAMBRIDGE STREET /
Straight forward, good natured breakfasts at this appealing little kitchen sitting on an appealing little Pimlico street. Come for the pancakes.
